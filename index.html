The Antifeed

News and editorials outside your comfort zone.

Online media has become an echo chamber. Algorithms designed by Facebook, Google, Twitter, et. al. to make it easier for you to get the information you want, also make sure you ONLY see information you like. Dissenting opinions and alternate viewpoints are hidden from your newsfeeds and search results.

We want to change that.

That's why we're building The Antifeed - A weekly newsletter, personally curated for you, using a comination of machine learning and human editorial experitse. The Antifeed will hit your inbox Sunday AM, giving you news and info you need to broaden your world view.
